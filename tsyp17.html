TSYP 2017 organisation

• Dec 15, 2017

“IEEE IAS ENIS Student Chapter participated in organizing the 2017 IEEE Tunisian Students & Young Professionals Congress (TSYP) which has been an amazing experience. It was a pleasure to meet attendees of the 2017 TSYP from different IEEE student branches in Sousse, Tunisia. We had a very successful conference in terms of attendees, technical sessions, student program, as well as committee’s meetings and social functions. We hope you all had a nice time at the conference where you were able to learn something new, renewed your networks, and created new networks and at the same time had some fun. The IEEE ENIS SB members would like to extend their sincere gratitude and express their love to all IEEE members and volunteers that were present in this edition of TSYP. It has always been a pleasure to meet with all of you. We congratulate IEEE INSAT Student Branch for winning the TSYP competition and wish them the best of luck in organizing the next TSYP congress. It has been a great honor for us to host this event, we look forward to seeing you again at next IEEE conferences.“
